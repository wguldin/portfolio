A little about me and my work

Hi, my name is Will Guldin and I work as the lead UI & UX designer at Britecore, a web-based app used by insurance companies. I've found there's a lot of differences in the design challenges we face compared to those faced by a more traditional website.

I started this blog to share some of the lessons we've learned along the way.

My interest with visual design started in the darkroom, as an art photographer in high school. In college, I studied journalism, and eventually found web design to be a good mix of technical and creative skills.

I love to cook, so in my spare time, I'm probably in the kitchen, with my wonderful wife, Megan.
